Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2024

Data Collection

At the Ontologic Design Institute, we believe in transparent and ethical data practices that align with our design principles. We collect only essential information needed to:

  • Provide and improve our educational services
  • Facilitate community engagement
  • Support research initiatives
  • Enable meaningful design discourse

How We Use Your Data

Your data helps us create more meaningful and personalized experiences within our design community. We use it to:

  • Customize your learning experience
  • Connect you with relevant research and projects
  • Improve our educational content and methodologies
  • Facilitate collaboration between members

Data Protection

We employ advanced security measures and ethical design principles to protect your information:

  • End-to-end encryption for sensitive data
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Strict access controls and authentication
  • Transparent data handling practices

Your Rights

You have complete control over your data, including the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Request data modification or deletion
  • Opt-out of data collection
  • Export your data in a portable format
