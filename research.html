Research at ODI

The Ontologic Design Institute conducts pioneering research at the intersection of consciousness, design, and emergent systems. Our interdisciplinary approach combines philosophy, cognitive science, and design theory to explore how conscious experience shapes and is shaped by design.

Current Research Initiatives

Consciousness Studies

Investigating the relationship between conscious experience and design principles through empirical research and philosophical inquiry.

Emergent Systems

Studying how complex design systems emerge from simple rules and how consciousness influences this emergence.
