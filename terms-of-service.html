Terms Overview

Welcome to the Ontologic Design Institute. These terms govern your interaction with our platform and community, emphasizing our commitment to ethical design practices and collaborative knowledge creation.

User Responsibilities

  • Engage respectfully with the design community
  • Contribute to meaningful discourse around ontological design
  • Maintain academic integrity in research contributions
  • Respect intellectual property rights

Intellectual Property

All content published through our platform is protected under Creative Commons Attribution-ShareAlike 4.0, promoting open knowledge sharing while maintaining attribution requirements.

Ethical Guidelines

Our community adheres to strict ethical guidelines in design research and practice, emphasizing:

  • Transparency in design processes
  • Accountability for design impacts
  • Inclusive and accessible design practices
  • Environmental consciousness in design solutions

Dispute Resolution

Any disputes will be resolved through our community-based mediation process, emphasizing dialogue and collaborative problem-solving aligned with our ontological design principles.
