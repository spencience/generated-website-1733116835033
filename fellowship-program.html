Fellowship Program

The Ontologic Design Institute Fellowship Program is a transformative year-long journey that brings together designers, philosophers, researchers, and practitioners to explore the deeper patterns and possibilities of systemic change through design.

Program Highlights

Immersive Learning

Engage in deep theoretical frameworks and practical applications of ontologic design principles.

Mentorship

Work closely with leading practitioners in design, philosophy, and systems thinking.

Research Projects

Develop and implement original research contributing to the field of ontologic design.

Global Community

Join a diverse network of practitioners committed to systemic transformation.

Fellowship Structure

  • 12-month commitment starting September 2024
  • Hybrid format combining virtual and in-person residencies
  • Individual research project development
  • Weekly seminars and workshops
  • Quarterly symposiums

How to Apply

We welcome applications from individuals with diverse backgrounds who are committed to exploring and advancing the field of ontologic design.
